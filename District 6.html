Welcome to Ernakulam


Ernakulam also known as Kochi is no exception for this. Ernakulam is the commercial capital and the most developed city of Kerala. This place has great significance in the history.Major portion of the district is from the kingdom of Kochi also called as the Queen of Arabian Sea. The district consists of the mainland Ernakulam, the man made Willington Island, Mattancherry, Fort Kochi, Vypeen Island, Bolgatty Palace etc.




Top Tourist Spots To Visit

The Mattancherry Museum

Centuries old architectural remains

Thattekkad Bird Sanctuary

Home to endangered migratory birds

Vypeen Island

Dotted with serene beaches and backwaters

Vasco House

One of the oldest Portugese houses

Bolgatty Palace

Oldest palace built by Dutch in India

Jewish synagogue

Contains giant scrolls of old testament

Fort Kochi Beach

Sight of Chinese fishing Nets and ships

Folklore museum

Exhibits cultural and tribal artefacts

Santa Cruz Basilica

Oldest cathedral and architectural marvel

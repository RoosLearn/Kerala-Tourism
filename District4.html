Welcome to Kozhikode!


Kozhikode will always have a special place in the history of Kerala as it is here that Vasco-da-Gama first landed and the legendary Spice Route came into existence. This region still has a historic charm to it. Kozhikode Beach is the personification of the numerous characteristics that make Kozhikode so unique. People flock in large numbers to view the sunset from the beach. One gets a fresh supply of seafood in the shacks that surround the place.

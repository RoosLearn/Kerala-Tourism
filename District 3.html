Welcome to Alappuzha!


Referred to as the Venice of the East, Alappuzha has always enjoyed an important place in the maritime history of Kerala. Today, it is famous for its boat races, backwater holidays, beaches, marine products and coir industry.Alappuzha Beach is a popular picnic spot.Another delightful experience while in Alappuzha is the houseboat cruise.




Top Tourist Spots To Visit

Alappuzha Beach

Sandy Beach with Houseboat tours
